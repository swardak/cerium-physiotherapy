Our Services

Physiotherapy

$95/hour

Our physiotherapists are constantly updating their skills and knowledge in orthopaedics and sports injury, allowing them to offer the best possible treatment, improved quality of care, and better results for their clients.

Massage Therapy

$65/hour

Massage Therapy involves the kneading and manipulation of soft tissues to gain a therapeutic response and is great for decreasing the development of painful muscular patterning after injury and for accelerating rate of recovery.

Sports Injury

$105/hour

Our team has specialized knowledge of musculoskeletal disorders to better treat injuries such as sprains, strains, ligament tears, cartilage problems, dislocations and fractures through sport/athletic therapy techniques.

Accupuncture

$45/30 min

Acupuncture can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, sciatica, back pain, bursitis and more. The technique involves inserting thin, disposable needles into specific acupuncture points throughout the body.

Concussion Rehab

$80/hour

Our approach to concussion care offers safe, drug-free treatments and has been proven to be highly effective.We can help address symptoms related to concussions, including headache, fatigue, poor concentration, and more.

Vestibular Rehab

$110/hour

Vestibular rehabilitation (VR), or vestibular rehabilitation therapy (VRT), is a form of therapy used to reduce the symptoms of vestibular disorders, such as vertigo, dizziness, balance issues and gaze instability.
